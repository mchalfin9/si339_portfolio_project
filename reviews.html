Course Reviews

Metropolis Country Club - Private

Location: White Plains, New York

Metropolis is arguably one of my favorite golf course, not becasue this is my home course, but it is very challenging despite being only 6,900 yards and a par 70 from the tips. The Course in always is fantastic condition and requires creativity. Metropolis held the NY State Open Qualifier in the summer of 2020.

Erin Hills - Public

Location: Hartford, Wisconsin

Erin Hills is one of the most well-known golf destinations in the entire world. Many people and golfers know it from the 2017 US Open, which if you do not know is a major professional tournament. Although the price to play at Erin Hills is not cheap, it is definetley worth it as it presents all the challenges the mid-west has to offer (windy, fast, firm).

Lawsonia Links - Public

Location: Green Lake, Wisconsin

Gaining popularity and recently breaking into the top 100 list, Lawsonia (the links course) is absolutely unreal. The course itself is very manageable but yet again there are many places that are punishing. The vibes are very chill and it is a course that is not going to make you work too hard for a casual round of golf.

Blackwolf River Course - Public

Location: Kohler, Wisconsin

The final winsconsin track I was bale to experience was the river course at Blackwolf Run. This course is unreal, as the river that the course is built around is seen on almost every hole. Although it was not the greatest weather, the course was really awesome. A truly greay landscape and feel that allows for all types of golfers to experience.

University of Michigan Golf Course - Public

Location: Ann Arbor, Michigan

The University of Michigan Golf Course is a MacKenzie design, same designer of Augusta National (home of the Masters Tournament). The course has some very interesting holes that personally I don't think are great. However, this course is great for U-M students to play on as it is a beautiful golf course at a great price.

Old Sandwich - Private

Location: Plymouth, Massachusetts

Old Sandwich Golf Club is one of my favorite courses in the country out of the ones I have played. Not only is the course just absolutely beautiful, it is very challenging and every hole is different. Also, the club is exclusive and it is great to play a course that is always pure because it is not getting that much traffic. OS is a top 100 golf course in the country and deserves to be. Also, I believe they have one of the best Pro Shops.

Fairview Country Club - Private

Location: Greenwich, Connecticut

Fairway is a course I have played many times and worked as a caddie for many years. In my opinion, the course used to be in better shape and harder, but they have made it easier due to complaints by the members. However, the course is very nice and has very tight fairways. When the greens are in good shape they can be extremely quick.

Willow Ridge Country Club - Private

Location: Harrison, New York

Willow Ridge is a private course that I have been able to experience various times for high school matches along with casual rounds of golf. This course is very beautiful and well maintained. It is not the hardest course and allows for all types of players to have fun.
